Join us for an exclusive evening at the Great Pyramids of Giza, where history, culture, and adventure come together for an unforgettable experience.

Date and Time

Sun, November 22 • 5:00 PM GMT+3

Location

Great Pyramids of Giza, Giza Plateau, Egypt

Agenda

1:00 PM – 2:30

Welcome & Registration

Guests arrive and register at the main entrance to the Pyramids, greeted with complimentary drinks and snacks while receiving their event passes.

2:30 PM – 3:30

Guided Historical Tour

A guided tour led by expert Egyptologists through the Great Pyramid (Khufu), Pyramid of Khafre, and Pyramid of Menkaure.

3:30 PM – 5:00

Dinner Under the Stars

A traditional Egyptian feast is served in a beautifully set outdoor dining area. Guests enjoy local cuisine while mingling with other attendees, surrounded by the grandeur of the Pyramids.

About this Event

This event invites history enthusiasts, tourists, and local residents to explore the ancient wonders, discover their untold stories, and enjoy a magical night filled with guided tours, live performances, and a unique blend of entertainment under the stars.
